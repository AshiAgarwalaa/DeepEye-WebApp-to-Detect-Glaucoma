Brief Description

The tool allows a user to input a fundus image and predict whether the given image is glaucomatic or non-glaucomatic. It works on deep neural networks and can be broadly divided in three major steps. The first and second step is to segment optic disc and optic cup respectively. The third step includes calculation of Cup-to-Disc Ratio which is then used to classify the given image.
